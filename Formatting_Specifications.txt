=== MAIN CONTENT FORMATTING ===

SECTION HEADERS (I, II, III, IV, V, VI, VII, VIII):
- Font: 14pt, Bold
- Alignment: Left
- Spacing: 2 line breaks before, 1 line break after
- Format: "I. УВОД", "II. ОБЗОР НА УЕБ ТЕХНОЛОГИИ", etc.

SUB-SECTION HEADERS:
- Font: 12pt, Bold  
- Alignment: Left
- Spacing: 1 line break before and after
- Format: "Цели", "Задачи", "Обхват", etc.

BODY TEXT:
- Font: 12pt, Regular
- Line Spacing: 1.5
- Alignment: Justified
- Paragraph Spacing: 6pt after each paragraph

BULLET POINTS:
- Use "•" symbol
- Indent: 1.25cm from left margin
- Format: "• Модерна архитектура базирана на ASP.NET Core..."

NUMBERED LISTS:
- Format: "1. Създаване на интуитивна система..."
- Indent: 1.25cm from left margin

CODE BLOCKS:
- Font: Courier New, 10pt
- Background: Light gray (RGB: 245, 245, 245)
- Border: 1pt solid gray
- Padding: 10pt
- Alignment: Left

FIGURE PLACEHOLDERS:
- Format: "Фиг. 1. Архитектура на ASP.NET Core"
- Font: 10pt, Italic
- Alignment: Center
- Spacing: 1 line break before and after

TABLE FORMATTING:
- Border: 1pt solid black
- Header Row: Bold, Background color: Light blue
- Cell Padding: 5pt
- Font: 11pt
